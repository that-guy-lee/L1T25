Who do I swim for?

Aqua Warriors

I swim for a club called Aqua Warriors in the south of Johannesburg.
Aqua Warriors is a year round swimming school with lessons ranging from learn to swim to advanced swimming classes for all ages.
I've been swimming with them for years, and they truly have been amazing.
They strive to provide the best training and development possible
